# EduMind 🎓
> A decentralized AI study assistant built on 0G infrastructure

## 🧠 What is EduMind?
EduMind is an AI-powered study assistant that lets students 
upload their lecture notes, PDFs, and study materials to 
0G's decentralized storage — then ask questions, get 
summaries, and generate practice quizzes using AI powered 
by 0G Compute.

No centralized servers. No corporate control. 
Your study data, owned by you.

---

## 🚨 The Problem
Most AI study tools like ChatGPT and Notion AI are 
centralized:
- Your data is stored on private corporate servers
- Subject to censorship, shutdown, or data breaches
- You have no true ownership of your content

---

## ✅ Our Solution
EduMind rebuilds the study assistant on a trustless, 
decentralized foundation using 0G's modular AI infrastructure.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. Student uploads notes/PDFs → stored on **0G Storage**
2. Student asks a question → processed by **0G Compute**
3. AI returns a verified, tamper-proof answer
4. All interactions are transparent and on-chain

---

## 🔑 Key Features
- 📁 Upload and store study materials on 0G Storage
- 💬 Ask AI questions about your uploaded content
- 📝 Generate summaries and practice quizzes
- 🔒 Fully decentralized — no central point of failure
- ✅ Verifiable AI responses via 0G Compute Network
